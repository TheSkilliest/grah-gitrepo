    FAQ

    Questions, answered.

    If something is still unclear, email us and we'll be happy to help.

    When is Graphicly launching?

    What is Graphicly?

    Graphicly is an AI-powered design platform that turns ideas into professional visual content through simple conversation.

    How does Graphicly work?

    Simply describe what you want to create. Graphicly generates the design, lets you refine it through conversation, and exports it in ready-to-use formats.

    Do I need any design skills?

    No. If you can describe an idea, you can create a design. There are no complicated tools, layers, or design software to learn.

    What can I create with Graphicly?

    Social media posts, carousels, ads, banners, presentations, educational content, business graphics, and much more.

    Can I start from templates?

    Yes. Start from a blank canvas or choose from a growing library of professionally designed templates that can be customized through conversation.

    Can I generate multiple variations at once?

    Yes. Generate multiple versions of a design, create content variations, or produce entire batches of graphics from a single prompt.

    Does Graphicly support multiple languages?

    Yes. You can create designs in virtually any language, including multilingual content and complex writing systems.

    Can it handle complex content and typography?

    Yes. Graphicly supports complex scripts, right-to-left languages, scientific equations, mathematical notation, technical content, and structured layouts.

    Can I use my own branding and assets?

    Yes. Upload your logos, images, brand colors, fonts, and other assets to keep every design consistent with your brand.

    What formats can I export?

    Designs can be exported as JPG, PNG, and PDF for social media, web, presentations, and print.

    Will new templates be added over time?

    Absolutely. Our template library will continue expanding with new templates, categories, industries, and use cases.

    How much will Graphicly cost?

    Pricing will be announced closer to launch. Our goal is to make Graphicly accessible and affordable for creators, marketers, businesses, and agencies.
